Testing Factory

At Pragma Tecnologias, we offer comprehensive Testing Factory services that ensure your software products and processes are of the highest quality. Our approach involves planning, defining, and executing a customized test strategy based on your specific requirements. We believe that quality should be a top priority for any software product, and we strive to deliver services that help our clients meet this objective.

Our Testing Factory services include a range of testing types to ensure that all aspects of your software are thoroughly evaluated. These testing types include:

  • Strategy: We define a test strategy that sets milestones, outlines what will be tested, who will be involved, and how the testing will be carried out.
  • Planning: We establish workloads, assign tasks, and manage resources to ensure that testing is completed on time and on budget.
  • Functional: We conduct testing that involves the business stakeholders to ensure that the software meets the business requirements.
  • System: We conduct testing of the application ecosystem to ensure that all components are working together correctly.
  • User: We conduct testing by the end-users to ensure that the software is easy to use, efficient, and effective.
  • Performance: We conduct stress and load testing to ensure that the software can handle large volumes of data and users.
  • Security: We conduct testing to identify vulnerabilities and address common security issues to ensure the security of your software product.

We understand that the success of your business depends on the quality of your software, and we are committed to providing the highest quality Testing Factory services to help you achieve your goals.
