Software Maintenance

At Pragma Tecnologias, we understand the importance of keeping your software systems running smoothly and efficiently. That's why we offer comprehensive software maintenance services that are designed to support the ongoing success of your business.

Our software maintenance services include corrective maintenance, adaptive maintenance, perfective maintenance, and preventive maintenance. These services are designed to address different aspects of software maintenance, from fixing bugs and errors to improving performance, adding new features, and proactively maintaining your software to prevent future issues. With our team of experienced software maintenance specialists, you can trust us to provide reliable, efficient, and effective software maintenance services, allowing you to focus on growing your business.

  • Corrective maintenance: This type of maintenance focuses on fixing any errors or bugs in your software. Our team will diagnose and resolve any issues to keep your systems running optimally.
  • Adaptive maintenance: This type of maintenance involves modifying your software to accommodate changes in your business environment, such as updates to operating systems or new software requirements. Our team will work with you to ensure your software remains adaptable and relevant.
  • Perfective maintenance: This type of maintenance is designed to improve the functionality and performance of your software. Whether you need new features added or existing ones enhanced, we will work with you to identify and implement the necessary changes.
  • Preventive maintenance: This type of maintenance focuses on proactively identifying and addressing potential problems before they arise. Our team will work with you to implement measures that help prevent issues from occurring in the first place, ensuring your software remains stable and secure.
