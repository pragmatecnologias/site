Staffing Services

At Pragma Tecnologias, we understand the challenges that businesses face when it comes to acquiring and retaining the right talent to fulfill their IT needs. That's why we offer a range of staffing services designed to help organizations quickly and effectively acquire the skilled IT resources they need to succeed.

Here are some of the staffing services we provide:

  • Contract Staffing: Our contract staffing service enables organizations to quickly acquire skilled IT resources on a temporary basis to manage fluctuating workloads and meet specific project needs. We provide you with the right talent to help your organization achieve its objectives.
  • Permanent Staffing: Our permanent staffing service is designed to help organizations identify, attract and hire the best talent to fulfill their IT needs on a long-term basis. We work with you to understand your business requirements and help you find the right fit for your organization.
  • Contract-to-Hire Staffing: Our contract-to-hire staffing service allows organizations to assess the performance of a contracted employee before making a long-term hiring decision. We provide you with the flexibility to try out an employee before making a long-term commitment.
  • Managed Services Staffing: Our managed services staffing provides organizations with a dedicated team of IT professionals to manage and maintain their IT infrastructure and applications. We help you reduce your IT costs and improve your operational efficiencies.
  • Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO): Our RPO service enables organizations to outsource their entire recruitment process to Pragma Tecnologias, from sourcing candidates to hiring and onboarding. We provide end-to-end recruitment services, so you can focus on your core business while we take care of your staffing needs.

With our staffing services, you can have the right people with the right skills at the right time to meet your business goals. Our experienced team can help you find the right solution to your staffing needs and provide the support you need to succeed.
